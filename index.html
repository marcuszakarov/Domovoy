The Challenge
Families choose the school first.
The home comes second.
A new chapter begins — finding the right home near the right school

Relocating a family is more than finding four walls.

Every year, hundreds of families relocate to Phuket for its international schools. They face a fragmented rental market, unverified listings, language barriers, and agents who don't understand what "near the school" actually means for a family's daily life.

The morning commute. The after-school activities. The weekend routine. The neighbourhood your children will call home. These details matter — and they're exactly what generic property platforms ignore.

We built Domovoy to solve this. Starting from the school your family has chosen, we match you with verified, family-suitable properties — in the right zone, at the right price, with transparent terms.

Our Approach
School-first. Family-first. Always.

We don't start with the property. We start with where your children will learn — and build everything around that.
